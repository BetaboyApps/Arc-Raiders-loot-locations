## Map Information

### Dam Battlegrounds (Level 15-25)
- Large industrial facility with hydroelectric infrastructure
- Key locations: Power Rod Room, Hydroelectric Control, Maintenance Tunnels
- Best for: Mid-level players, consistent loot runs

### Acceria Spaceport (Level 25-35)
- Massive orbital facility with multiple launch bays
- Key locations: Vehicle Maintenance Bay, Launch Bay Alpha, Command Center
- Best for: High-level players, advanced equipment

### Buried City (Level 10-20)
- Underground urban ruins with hidden passages
- Key locations: Secret Apartment, Underground Arsenal, Ancient Library
- Best for: Beginners, safe loot runs

### Blue Gate (Level 30+)
- Experimental facility with portal technology
- Key locations: Maintenance Wing Breach, Portal Chamber, Research Labs
- Best for: Experienced players, experimental gear

### Stella Montis (Level 25-35)
- Frozen underground research complex
- Key locations: Assembly Rocket Thrusters, Medical Research Wing, Seed Vault
- Best for: Advanced players, unique materials
